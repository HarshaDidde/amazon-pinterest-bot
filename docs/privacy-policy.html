Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how HD Finds ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our Pinterest application and affiliated services.

1. What We Do

We operate an automated service that publishes Pinterest pins linking to publicly available best-selling products on Amazon Canada. We participate in the Amazon Associates Program and may earn a commission on qualifying purchases made through our links.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from Pinterest users. Our application interacts with the Pinterest API solely to create pins on our own Pinterest boards. We do not request access to your Pinterest account, followers, messages, or any personal data.

The only data we store internally is a log of the product ASINs and Pinterest pin URLs we have already posted, used to prevent duplicate posts. This data is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to us.

3. Pinterest API Usage

Our application uses the Pinterest API v5 under the Pinterest Developer Terms of Service. We use the API only to create pins on our own boards using the pins:write scope. We do not read, store, or process any data belonging to other Pinterest users.

4. Amazon Associates Disclosure

We are a participant in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising programme designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.ca. When you click a link in our pins and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This privacy policy page itself does not use cookies or any tracking scripts. Amazon and Pinterest may use their own cookies when you visit their platforms; please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.
